## What is Agile?

Agile is an iterative approach to software development emphasizing flexibility, collaboration, and customer feedback over rigid planning.

## The Agile Manifesto

| Value | Over |
|-------|------|
| Individuals and interactions | Processes and tools |
| Working software | Comprehensive documentation |
| Customer collaboration | Contract negotiation |
| Responding to change | Following a plan |

## Scrum Framework

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Product Owner** | Defines features, prioritizes backlog |
| **Scrum Master** | Facilitates process, removes blockers |
| **Development Team** | Builds the product (self-organizing) |

**Ceremonies**:
- **Sprint Planning**: What will we build this sprint? (2 weeks)
- **Daily Standup**: What did I do? What will I do? Blockers?
- **Sprint Review**: Demo completed work to stakeholders
- **Retrospective**: What went well? What can improve?

## Kanban

Focuses on continuous delivery and workflow visualization:

```
To Do | In Progress | Review | Done
  📝       🔧          🔍      ✅
  T3       T1          T2      T4
  T5       T6                  T7
```

**Work In Progress (WIP) limits** prevent bottlenecks.
